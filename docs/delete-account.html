Account Deletion — Seller Helper AI for eBay

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Seller Helper AI for eBay account and the data associated with it. See also the privacy policy.

How to request deletion

Email jochoa105@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my Seller Helper account", sent from the Google account you use to sign in to the app (this is how the request is verified). No other information is needed. Deletion requests are completed within 30 days, and you will receive a confirmation reply when it is done.

What is deleted

  • Your sign-in record (Google account email and identifier) held in Firebase Authentication.
  • Your token balance and per-listing spend records held in Google Firestore. Any unused tokens are forfeited when the account is deleted.

What is not affected

  • Data on your device (item entries, photos, eBay connection tokens) never leaves your control — delete it by removing entries in the app or uninstalling the app. Disconnecting your eBay account in Settings removes its tokens from your device.
  • Listings you published on eBay belong to your own eBay account and are managed through eBay (Seller Hub) — the app has no server-side copy of them.
