1 Interests

This section will talk a little about some of my interests, whether they be professional or hobbies.

First let's start with the professional interests. I'm a trained software engineer (SE) so most of my interests reside in that area. I said trained because i haven't gotten a job in that line of work yet but i'm still looking. What i specially interest me about SE is the security aspect. I think it is really easy to disregard that part of the software specially when the clients don't know the implications of having a vulnerable system.

Another big interest of mine is in Artificial Intelligence (AI). Many smart people (Elon Musk, Stephen Hawkings, etc) have shown concern about AI and i can see why. To put in in simple terms, there are some AI’s that are indistinguishable from a human mind and they keep improving. Some AIs us game theory to achieve their goals and, depending on the AI, this goals could be malicious.

As for non-technical interests I also play guitar and piano on my free time. After a good day of coding i haven't been able to find a more soothing exercise than to get lost on my instruments and just play until i fall asleep.

3 Personal Projects

3.1 Natural Selection Project

Im making a mobile app dedicated to teaching Darwinian Natural Selection in a fun way. The app will have you operating the climate as the main character and the objective is to eliminate the organisms that will keep popping up on the field.

The idea is to show the player how hard it is to eradicate living organisms when they evolve and adjust to the environment, in this case your attacks.

The game is still in the planning phase. Will post updates as they come. If you want to subscribe to updates you can subscribe to the mail list to recieve updates.
