Privacy Policy — Seller Helper AI for eBay

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Seller Helper AI for eBay ("the app") helps you photograph a secondhand item, analyze it with AI, and publish it as a listing on your own eBay account. This policy describes what data the app handles and where it goes. The app is developed by an independent developer and is not affiliated with or endorsed by eBay Inc.

Information the app collects

  • Google account (sign-in): if you sign in, we receive your Google account email address and identifier via Firebase Authentication. This identifies your token balance (tokens are used to publish listings).
  • Photos: photos you take or pick are stored on your device. They are sent to Google's Gemini AI service (via Firebase) to analyze the item, and uploaded to eBay's picture hosting service when you publish a listing.
  • eBay account connection: when you connect your eBay account, eBay issues the app access tokens that are stored only on your device. The app uses them to create listings on your behalf when you explicitly tap Publish — never on its own.
  • Listing details: item details (title, description, item specifics, price), your ZIP code, and package weight are stored on your device and sent to eBay as part of the listings you publish.
  • Token balance and purchase records: your token balance and per-listing spend records are stored in Google Firestore, tied to your signed-in account.

What the app does not do

  • No advertising and no ad tracking.
  • Your data is never sold or shared with third parties beyond the services named above (Google Firebase and eBay), which are used solely to provide the app's functionality.
  • No background activity on your eBay account — listings are created only on your explicit action.

Data retention and deletion

Item entries and photos live on your device and are deleted when you delete them or uninstall the app. Disconnecting your eBay account removes its tokens from your device. To delete your account data on our servers (sign-in record, token balance, spend history), email the address below from your signed-in Google account and it will be removed.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and requires an eBay account to publish.
